About Us

Queen's Mushroom is a producer of fresh and processed oyster mushrooms established in 2022 in Tanjungpinang, Riau Islands. We are driven by a passion to deliver fresh, healthy, and sustainable mushroom products of the highest quality...

To become a trusted and leading producer of fresh and processed oyster mushrooms in local, regional, and international markets...

Produce high-quality and sustainable mushrooms, create innovative mushroom-based products for export, and build strong partnerships with local farmers...
